Georgia

We are a travel company established by travellers for travellers, based at Tbilisi, Georgia.

The gem of Caucasia.


Georgia is a country in the Caucasus region of Eurasia, Located at the crossroads of Western Asia and Eastern Europe, it is bounded to the west by the Black sea,to the north by Russia, to the south by Turkey and Armenia, and to the southeast by Azerbaijan. The Capital and largest city is Tbilisi. It covers a territory of 69,700 square kilometres (26,911 sq mi), and its population is about 4 million. Georgia is a unitary, semi-Presidential republic, with the government elected through a representative democracy.

The Georgian hospitality is legendary,native Georgians are friendly and welcoming. Georgia is one of the safest places in Europe and Asia in terms of crimes and safety of women. Georgia has a rich history and culture,also happened to be in the silk route!

Mount Kazbek

Shio-Mgvime Monastery

"The cave of Shio" is a medieval monastic complex in Georgia, near the town of Mtskheta

Beautiful Kazbegi

Just one of the extraordinary landscapes among many in the Kazbegi, Georgia

Agmashenebeli Avenue

One of the main avenues in the historical part of Tbilisi, known for its 19th-century classical architecture

Parliament Building

Promoted as a symbol of Georgia's bright, democratic future it is the home of the Parliament of Georgia since its inauguration on May 26, 2012

Dry Bridge Flea Market

This extraordinary flea market will keep you mesmerised with an assortment of antiques, jewellery and bric-a-brac on sale

Chronicles of Georgia

One of the more prominent historical monuments of Georgia, it is a view to behold

Narikala Fort

Narikala is an ancient fortress overlooking Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, and the Kura River

Uplistsikhe

Uplistsikhe is an ancient rock-hewn town in eastern Georgia, some 10 kilometers east of the town of Gori, Shida Kartli

Alaverdi Monastery

A Georgian Eastern Orthodox monastery, in the Kakheti region of Eastern Georgia, built in the 11th century by Kvirike III of Kakheti

Gergeti Trinity Church

The Holy Trinity Church near the village of Gergeti in Georgia, is situated at an elevation of 2170 meters, under Mount Kazbegi

Bagrati Monastery

The Cathedral of the Dormition, commonly known as Bagrati Cathedral, is an 11th-century cathedral in the city of Kutaisi
