What to do?

For the traveller Georgia has many options:
A nature's lover has plenty of diverse landscapes,including high mountains,plains,rivers,lakes and the Black sea. Expansive apple orchards, many citrus orchards and vineyards adorn the countryside!

For a history and heritage lover, the monasteries and churches, forts and museums spread across the country offers a glimpse into the bygone days. Remember the famous historical"silk route" passes through Georgia.

For a leisure break one has beautiful mountain or sea resorts, casinos and night clubs.

For the adventurous ones river rafting, mountain trekking, paragliding or winter sports are all within 69700 km2!
